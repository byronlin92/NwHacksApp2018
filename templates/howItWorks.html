Let Our Service Reach You

Our service aims to connect you with service work or repairs that you might require at your home, without having to go through the long, expensive routes of having to obtain a professional service.

There are many ordinary individuals in your community who have the skills and expertise to handle some of your housekeeping and maintenance needs. Our service allows you to connect with these people based on your location, and furthermore ensures that the people we connect you with are verified in their field of work.

Our application opens with a view of your surroundings, showing you the various services and people available nearby. Through our booking service, you will be able to connect with these people and continue with the scheduling of their work. Payments and labour laws must be followed, and can be made in person to the service attendee.

If you would like to contact any of the staff at Help Me Clean, feel free to call (778) 111 1111 or send us an email at information@helpmeclean.com. One of our customer service representatives will get back to you as quick as we can!
